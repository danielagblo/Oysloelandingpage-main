Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

How do I create an account on OYSLOE?

Creating an account is easy! Simply click the "Sign Up" button, enter your phone number, and follow the verification process. You'll receive a verification code via SMS to complete your registration.

What are the fees for selling on OYSLOE?

OYSLOE charges a 5% transaction fee on all sales for standard sellers. Premium sellers enjoy a reduced rate of 3%. Payment processing fees may also apply depending on your chosen payment method.

How do I list my first product?

To list your first product, go to your seller dashboard, click "Add New Listing," upload high-quality photos, write a detailed description, set your price, and choose your shipping options. Your listing will be reviewed and published within 24 hours.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept multiple payment methods including mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. All payments are processed securely through our trusted payment partners.

How do I handle returns and refunds?

Buyers can request returns within 7 days of receiving their order. Sellers are responsible for setting their own return policies. OYSLOE provides dispute resolution services to help resolve conflicts between buyers and sellers.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take your privacy and security seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through multiple channels: email at support@bricsky.com, phone at +233 30 395 9615, WhatsApp, or through the live chat feature in our mobile app. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Can I sell services on OYSLOE?

Currently, OYSLOE focuses on physical products. However, we're working on expanding to include service listings in the future. Stay tuned for updates!
